Document ID: WF-OPS-002
Title: Fraud Investigation Policy
Version: 1.0

Purpose
This policy defines procedures for handling suspected fraudulent banking activity while minimizing financial loss and protecting customer accounts.

Scope
Applies to:
- Debit cards
- Credit cards
- Online Banking
- Wire Transfers
- Mobile Banking

Investigation Procedure

Authenticate customer identity.

Collect:
- Date
- Amount
- Merchant
- Channel
- Customer observations

Review:
- Device history
- Login history
- Recent beneficiary additions
- Transaction velocity
- Geographic anomalies

Decision Criteria

High Risk:
- Foreign transaction
- Multiple failed logins
- High value transfers
- Device mismatch

Medium Risk:
- New merchant
- Multiple OTP attempts

Low Risk:
- Merchant clarification required

Escalation Matrix

Immediate escalation required for:
- Identity theft
- Account takeover
- International fraud
- Wire fraud

Compliance Notes

Never disclose investigation details.
Never accuse customers of fraud.
Never guarantee reimbursement.
