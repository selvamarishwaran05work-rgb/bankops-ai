Document ID: WF-OPS-003
Title: Chargeback Processing Policy
Version: 1.0

Purpose
Defines procedures for processing customer disputes relating to card transactions.

Investigation Steps

1. Verify customer identity.
2. Verify transaction exists.
3. Determine dispute reason.
4. Check merchant response.
5. Validate supporting evidence.
6. Initiate chargeback if eligible.

Chargeback Reasons

- Duplicate transaction
- Goods not received
- Unauthorized transaction
- Incorrect amount
- Cancelled service

Escalation

Escalate if:
- Merchant disputes evidence
- Amount exceeds policy threshold
- Legal notice received

Compliance

Do not guarantee successful recovery.
