Document ID: WF-OPS-004
Title: Know Your Customer Verification Policy

Purpose

Ensure regulatory compliance through customer identity verification.

Required Documents

- Government ID
- Address Proof
- PAN or Tax ID
- Photograph

Enhanced Due Diligence

Required when:
- High value customers
- Politically Exposed Persons
- High-risk jurisdictions

Escalation

Missing documentation.
Document mismatch.
Identity verification failure.

Compliance

Never approve incomplete KYC.
