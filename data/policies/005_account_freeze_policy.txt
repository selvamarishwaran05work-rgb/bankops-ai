Document ID: WF-OPS-005
Title: Account Freeze Investigation Policy

Purpose

Defines procedures for account restriction investigations.

Possible Reasons

- Court order
- Regulatory request
- Fraud detection
- AML monitoring
- Customer request

Investigation

Review:
- Freeze source
- Freeze duration
- Regulatory notes
- Customer history

Escalation

Legal Team
Compliance Team
Fraud Team

Compliance

Do not remove freezes without authorization.
