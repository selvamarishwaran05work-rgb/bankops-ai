Document ID: WF-OPS-006
Title: Customer Dispute Resolution Policy

Purpose

Guide customer service representatives through dispute handling.

Workflow

Receive complaint.

Collect evidence.

Review transactions.

Consult applicable policies.

Determine recommendation.

Escalate when necessary.

Communication

Explain findings clearly.

Avoid assumptions.

Do not speculate.

Compliance

Maintain complete audit trail.

Protect customer privacy.

Never disclose confidential internal investigation notes.
